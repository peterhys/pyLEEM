title: Calibration summary
---

When operate with analyzer at dispersive plane, the image is typically analyzed by determine the line profile. The detector is
rated at 7 eV total, and the pixel values can be converted to the energy range. Typically, the pixel per eV value is 166 pixel/eV.

However, detailed calibration should be created due to different mirror settings and the path of the value.
